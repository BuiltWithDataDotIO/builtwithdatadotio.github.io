What data does pathfindersAI use?

BLS job study

Utilizing data from the BLS provides valuable, reliable insights into job trends, salaries, and industry growth, empowering individuals to make well-informed career decisions.

BLS Wage Statistics

Data from the BLS wage study is invaluable for understanding earnings trends across various occupations and industries, helping individuals and businesses make informed decisions about salaries and financial planning.

Career OneStop

Using data from CareerOneStop provides comprehensive and up-to-date information on job requirements, skills, and training opportunities, making it an essential resource for career planning and advancement.

Projections Central

Data from Projections Central offers critical insights into future job demand and industry growth, helping individuals and organizations strategically plan for emerging opportunities in the labor market.

How does pathfindersAI work for your learners?

Holla @mrareweb if you've got more questions and we'll do our best to answer.

  • Choose a Starting Place

    Learners can either begin their journey with a specific career in mind or leverage advanced AI features to discover and identify an ideal starting point based on their unique interests and skills.

  • View Profiles

    Learners are shown recommended careers that match their interests or skills.

  • Explore Careers

    Learners get detailed insight into career opportunities: salaries, required education, job growth, top employers, in-demand skills, and more.

  • Take the next step!

    Learners can also create a profile and quickly craft a career pathway. pathfindersAI will even suggest tasks and skills to include based on the job.
